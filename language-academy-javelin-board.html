Strategic Foundation & Testing Roadmap

This board outlines the validation criteria for a language acquisition web app that prioritizes Cultural Immersion for the Soul and a Rigorous 4-Skill Routine. The core mission is to empower the learner's success through rapid alignment to Canada's cultural and social context—achieving in weeks and months what traditionally would unnecessarily take years and decades. By removing cognitive friction through our Frictionless UI (inline translation, sentence blocks), enforcing a Rigorous 4-Skill Routine (90% self-study vs 10% tutor meetups), and utilizing GenAI Support for enriched assistance, we help guests and newcomers achieve deep cultural alignment far beyond basic functional frameworks.

Who is your customer? Be as specific as possible.
Time Limit: 5 Min
Customer
A learner who knows they need discipline but finds themselves "cheating" by only doing easy listening on other apps.
A learner who feels infantilized by "Cat and Dog" beginner books and wants to feel like an adult in Canada.
A learner who understands their boss but feels like an outsider when coworkers laugh at a joke or discuss the news.
A newcomer who is terrified of signing a rental contract or talking to a banker because they don't know the local rules.
An expert in their field who feels like they lose their personality and intelligence when they speak English at a high level.
A learner who wants to read real stories but finds context-switching to a dictionary app frustrating and exhausting.
A learner who skips writing tasks entirely because of the high effort required or because the prompt feels irrelevant.
What is the problem? Phrase it from your customer's perspective.
Time Limit: 5 Min
Problem
"I want to speak and write well, but without real accountability, I always take the path of least resistance and never improve."
"I feel like a child studying materials that don't reflect the real world I see outside my window every day."
"I am socially isolated because functional English isn't enough to grasp the informal mass culture everyone around me shares."
"I am scared I'll get scammed or make a social mistake because I don't understand how Canadian systems (housing, banking) actually work."
"I sound like a textbook, not a leader. I can't express complex opinions or sarcasm, so I stay quiet in high-level conversations."
"Reading a real article feels like a chore because I spend more time in a dictionary than actually enjoying the story."
"I don't write because I feel stuck without immediate help, and waiting days for a human to check my grammar makes the work feel useless."
Define the solution only after you have validated a problem worth solving.
Time Limit: 5 Min
Solution
A routine that "forces" active practice through varied tasks (standard emails vs. creative stories), unlocking tutor sessions as a reward.
Foundational skills taught through recognizable media (e.g. pop lyrics, authentic local narratives) that feels adult and relevant.
Interactive modules based on mass-market culture (TV clips, sports, regional news) to bridge the social gap.
Modules focused on practical navigation (dissecting rental agreements, neighborhood news) to build confidence and security.
Reading and debate modules centered on complex national issues and resonant literature to help find a "true voice."
A frictionless UI with invisible tools (inline click, sentence blocks, ElevenLabs) that lets me stay immersed in the story.
An AI assistant that provides instant suggestions and error-finding while I write, making the task feel supported rather than lonely.
